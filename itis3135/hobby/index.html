What

  • Cosplay is another way to express yourself and emulate a piece of media that might have left an impact on you.
  • Some people use 3D printing and others my use cosplay foam.
  • 3D printing is a way to create physical objects from a digital file which can be very expensive.
  • Foam is much cheaper and easier to work with. It can be cut, shaped, and painted to create a wide variety of costumes and props.
  • For both methods I would recommend looking up some guides on youtube. There are many creators with the same interests as you.

Why

  • Because its fun! Everyone has a form of media that inspires them. Being able to express your appreciation through cosplaying is a awesome feeling.
  • Its a way to find your people as well. Going into spaces where your interests align is a great way to make friends.
  • Even Sports fans do it. Wearing jersey's, facepaint, costumes of the team, and simply showing their passion in something they are interested in.

How

  • The first thing you have to do is find what you are interested it. There are many forms of media to emulate, but some impact you more than others.
  • After identifying what you like, looking up some guides online goes a long way. Many people have the same interests as you and are very talented.
  • Finally, identify how much you are willing to spend. There are budget cosplay's that put more expensive one's to shame. I've seen some pretty good cardboard cosplay's.
  • Remember, it can take some time to fully build out your outfit. Especially if you have multiple parts in it.
