Graphic Design Site Project

Project Overview
  • This application should take in Customer commissions and send them to the artist to design. Its purpose is to give life to people's imaginations and artistic endeavors for a low price.
  • This site strives to help local businesses be able to express and advertise their goods and services in a unique way.
  • The Overview of the site should include the ability to enter a commission, selecting from design choices, and getting in touch with the designer.
Site Map
Page Design
  • Home: This will contain basic overview of site goals and origin.
  • Services: Lists in detail of the services provided to customers.
  • Products: Explains the products the organization has available and what they are commonly used for.
  • Information: This page will give information on the history of the field the organization takes part in.
  • About: This will contian the organization's mission goal and what it hopes to achieve.
  • How to Join: Will be a apply for those who might be interested in working for the organization.
