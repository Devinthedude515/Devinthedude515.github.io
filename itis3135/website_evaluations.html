Website Evaluations

This site is to inform readers on the Basic CRAP principles in front-end development using HTML.

CRAP stands for Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, and Proximity.

Below are two websites that I have evaluated based on these principles.

Good Website
  • The contrast is simple black lettering on white background. Makes for easy accessiblity.
  • The site keeps a consistent model as you navigate through to other pages within it.
  • Alignment structure is very good and is consistent all the way through.
  • Though some items are very close together, most of the items are large enough to stand out and not give a "too close" feel.

Bad Website
  • The contrast on this site is not terrible, however the green against the blue can cause accessiblity concerns.
  • There is no clear structure to this site as it changes with each and every input.
  • The alignment is centered at first, but as you progress you start to find more items that are not aligned.
  • Everything is very close together and the site does not utilize much of the space it has.
